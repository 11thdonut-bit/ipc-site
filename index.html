System Profile: Soil Properties Assessment Resistance Thermal Analysis toolkit [SPARTA]

The SPARTA toolkit is a high-precision instrument designed for the complex requirements of regolith analysis on alien worlds. Having been developed and tested rigorously at the Jet Propulsion Laboratory in Southern California over the past several years, the SPARTA toolkit is based on published science. It provides the cost effectiveness and scientific prowess necessary to establish the space economy of the 21st century on the Moon, Mars, and beyond.
